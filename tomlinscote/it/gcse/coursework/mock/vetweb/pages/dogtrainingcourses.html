Dog training courses

Dog training courses are run every month.

They take place at one of three locations:

The Barn

The Hall

The Sports Club

Courses cost no more than £50 including a training collar.

Costs vary with location and the number of dogs on the course.

The maximum number of dogs on a course is 15.

The course consists of 8 sessions.

During the course your dog will learn to sit, stay, lie down, walk to heel without pulling on the lead, and come when you call even when other dogs are present.

All dogs have to be fully vaccinated before they can be accepted on the course.

Contact Rachel at the surgery to book your place.
